Sponsorship Opportunities

Join us in supporting future innovators by becoming a sponsor. Your contribution directly impacts students in the robotics field, providing them with resources, equipment, and experiences.

E-transfer

E-transfers can be sent to: sponsors@simbotics.org

We ask that you put your company’s name in the memo for easier identification.

Through School Board - School Cash Online

You can reach the school donation page by clicking the image below:

The information needed for this form will be:

  • The amount: Format the amount as:
    • 1114
    • 1114.00

    Do not use symbols such as “$” or “,”.

  • Under School, select “Governor Simcoe Secondary School”.
  • Under Initiative, select “Program Specific Donation”.

Below, there is an option to leave a message. Please specify “For Robotics Team”.

When using this method, you will receive a donation receipt automatically. This initial receipt will not be valid for sponsorship purposes. Our team will follow up with a valid business receipt.

Upon clicking “Add To Cart”, you should see the following details:

  • The correct school: Governor Simcoe Secondary School
  • Initiative: Program Specific Donation
  • The amount you entered

After proceeding, you may either sign in or continue as a guest. For simplicity, we recommend continuing as a guest, but creating a profile is also an option for future donations. Complete the checkout by providing your details.

Cheque

When writing a cheque, please make it payable to “Garden City Robotics Youth Organization Inc.”

To deliver the cheque, we typically arrange for a student or mentor to coordinate collection. Alternatively, you may mail it to:
